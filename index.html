About the Bride of the day

Adaeze Richards grew up in the Bay Area, spending much of her childhood in Palo Alto and Cupertino. She studied and worked in New York for two years before going to get her MBA in Fontainebleau and Singapore. Today, she works as an associate at Washburne Ventures in London

The Groom

About the Groom of the day

Ifeyani Obi was born in Nigeria and spent much of his life in Nigeria, moving to America at the age of 18 to study fine art. His work is known for blurring the line between technology and art and has been selected for display at exhibitions in Paris, Shanghai, and New York.
